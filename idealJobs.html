Jacopo's - Ideal Job

Ideal Job In IT

My Ideal job in IT would be Frontend Web Developer/Designer.

My goal is to work as a web developer and designer. This would allow me to use both my creative background and what I will learn during this course of study.

A front-end developer/designer is required to both convert data to graphical interfaces (Web development) and create visual designs, animations and graphics (Web design). Programming skills, as well as a good understanding of the social media environment, are required for this position. Moreover, creativity and good communication skills seem to be extremely relevant to this role. Successful candidates would ideally have an IT background, with programming experience, paired with the ability to create graphic designs and concepts with Adobe CC.

It is very important for me, after a career in the arts, to be able to work in a position that can express my creative skills while applying new programming skills necrssary in the IT world.

Nicholas's - Ideal Job

Ideal Job In IT

My Ideal job in IT would be a "Front End Web developer".

I believe at this point in time, I would like to be a 'Front End Web Developer', but specifically focused within the marketing industry.

Dilate Digital is a digital marketing agency, which focuses on implementing digital business marketing strategies to enable clients to reach their targeted audience effectively. They effectively work on four fundamental principles discover, build, launch and grow. They are looking for someone to implement and build highly functional and clean websites that are fundamentally SEO (Search Engine Optimised). This allows the business to show as the top results in search engines such a Google, Bing and Yahoo through the use of using keywords.

Throughout my studies at RMIT, I hope to develop my skill set to allow me to work within this industry.
