About me

Hi everyone! My name is Jacopo, I am 26 and I live in Sydney. I work as a professional dancer for Sydney Dance Company, and I have been dancing professionally for almost 9 years now. Before moving to Australia, two years and a half ago, I worked for several dance companies and theatres around Europe, in Italy, Germany and Poland. I am fluent in Italian, Spanish and English, and speak some German. I absolutely love hiking, baking and animals, especially cats.
